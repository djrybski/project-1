    Dan Rybski

    Early Life

    I was born on November 9, 1995 at Michael Reese Hospital. I was raised on the southside of Chicago in the Clearing neighborhood. My father, Paul, and my mother, Rita,work for the City's water department. I am the middle of three children. I attended St. Symphorosa Catholic grade school from Kindergarten to Eighth grade.

    Highschool

    I spent my first two years of highschool at Mount Carmel on Chicago's southside where I played baseball and soccer. I was a member of video production club where I learned that I had a passion and talent for video editing. It comes natural to me and I have since shot over 20 music videos for Chicagoland musicians. After my second year at Mount Carmel my parents decided that the hour commute everyday was too much so I transferred to Nazareth Academy in Lagrange Park.

    College

    I accepted an offer to play baseball at Indiana Tech University in Fort Wayne, Indiana. I spent two years in Fort Wayne as a computer science major until I realized that I did not want to be a programmer anymore. I wanted to go into technology consulting and decided to transfer to Illinois Institute of Technology where I would major in Information, technology and management.
